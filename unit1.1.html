Unit 1 1.1 Describe The purpose of I.T in Modern Business

The use of I.T IS important as most modern businesses now require the use of it to provide the relevant and important information for their companies growth and success towards other companies [1] The use of I.T now has befriended a range of companies both big and small. Even today with growth of modern farming, the use of I.T and computers is key to farmers as they have backups for their records, they know which animal is sick and requires medical help and if their produce has reached their end destination (to factories ready for the supermarket etc).

It also acts as a communication hub where the members of the business can collective work in a pair without the trouble of meeting up every so often to discuss plans and so on.
